# AI Engineer Job Market — 2025-2026 Research

> **Source:** Web research + Anthropic engineering blog + LinkedIn job market analysis
> **Date researched:** June 2026

---

## Key Trends

### 1. "Agentic AI" is the defining differentiator
- LinkedIn jobs specifically mention **"Agentic AI"** in titles (e.g., Zillow, Attis $250K)
- Multi-agent systems, tool-use, and autonomous workflows are the hottest skills
- Companies want engineers who can design agentic systems, not just call LLM APIs

### 2. "Applied AI Engineer" vs "ML Engineer" split
- **AI Engineer:** Builds AI-powered applications, integrates LLMs, designs agent systems, production deployment
- **ML Engineer:** Trains models, fine-tuning, data pipelines, ML infrastructure
- Plum's role is squarely in the "AI Engineer" category

### 3. Portfolio differentiators that impress
| Differentiator | Why It Matters |
|----------------|----------------|
| **Working multi-agent system** | Shows architectural thinking beyond prompt engineering |
| **Observability from day one** | Proves production readiness mindset |
| **Evaluation framework** | Demonstrates that you think about reliability |
| **Graceful degradation** | Shows you handle real-world failures |
| **Explainable decisions** | Critical for regulated industries (insurance) |
| **Cost-aware design** | LLM calls cost money — show you care |

### 4. What top companies look for
- **xAI, Anthropic, Google:** Focus on evaluation, safety, and building reliable AI
- **Healthtech (Plum-like):** Explainability, audit trails, compliance (ISO 27001, SOC 2)
- **Startups ($170K-$400K):** End-to-end ownership, shipped products, agentic architectures
